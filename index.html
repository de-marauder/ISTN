About Us

A health-inclined cereal brand

Our Vision

Our vision is for ISTN cereal to become a stable wholesome family meal in Nigeria and beyond. We are dedicated to providing families with healthy organic grains easily sourced from our country Nigeria.

Most importantly we want children and the elderly to no longer rely on foreign imported goods as their primary source of nutrition.

When you eat ISTN cereal, you are eating your way toward better health!

Our Approach

The need for healthy meals that are nutritious and affordable is a challenge that many families face. We believe that every family can at least have a complete nutritious whole meal in a day.

A meal comprising of healthy organic grains easily sourced from our country Nigeria.

ISTN cereal is an adaptation of the widely researched and internationally acclaimed Tom Brown cereal that is rich in protein, iron, carbohydrate, calcium, magnesium, copper, potassium, vitamin B6 and B12.

Our Process

ISTN is a family-owned cereal company based in Nigeria. Our mission is to create the need for every household to have a complete nutritious meal in a day. We do this by sourcing the finest blend of organic grains from our country, Nigeria, making it easy for everyone to get a balanced meal.

We believe that malnutrition isn’t just an issue for those in developing nations—it’s an issue for everyone. That’s why we work to ensure that everyone has access to healthy food so they can live up to their full potential.
